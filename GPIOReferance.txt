GPIO PINOUT
================



ac plug 1: pin 11 - GPIO 17 - success
ac plug 2: pin 16 - GPIO 23 - success
ac plug 3: pin 12 - GPIO 18 - success
ac plug 4: pin 15 - GPIO 22 - success
ac plug 5: pin 13 - GPIO 27 - fail (possibly bad relay switch)
ac plug 6: pin 7  - GPIO 4  - success

usb 3: pin 10 - GPIO 15 - success
